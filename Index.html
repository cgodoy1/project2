Life After NBA Draft

Ben Simmons was born July 20, 1996 to Dave and Julie Simmons in Melbourne, Australia. When he was 10 he played for the Knox Raiders. The next 6 years he chose basketball over football and moved to Montverde Florida to play basketball for Montverde Academy. In his senior season Simmons averaged 28.0 points, 11.9 rebounds, 4.0 assists and 2.6 steals and committed to playing college basketball for LSU. In his single college season he averaged 19.2 points, 11.8 rebounds, 4.8 assists, 2.0 steals a game. He entered the 2016 NBA draft and was selected #1 overall.
.
Unfortunately he broke his ankle in training camp and was out for the 2016-2017 NBA season. It was in the 2017-2018 NBA season he made his debut. In his first NBA season he averaged 15.8 points, 8.1 rebounds, 8.2 assist, 1.7 steals, and 0.9 blocks.
